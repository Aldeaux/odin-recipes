Enchiladas

Description

Chicken Enchiladas made from scratch juicy, cheesy and full of flavour! Theres a few components to homemade enchiladas, but it is absolutely worth the effort with flavour you can never achieve with packet spice mixes and bottled enchilada sauce.

Ingredients

  • Enchilada spice mix
  • Enchilada sauce and filling

Steps

  1. Make enchilada sauce
  2. Sear the chicken filling
  3. Fill the enchilada and roll it up
